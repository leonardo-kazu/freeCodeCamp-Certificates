Carol Shaw

One of the first female game developer

Timeline of Carol's life:

  • 1955 - Borned in Palo Alto, California
  • 1977 - Graduated from University of California, Berkley with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science
  • 1978 - Completed a master's degree in Computer Science at University of California, Berkley
  • 1978 - Hired at Atari, Inc
  • 1978 - Published 3-D Tic-Tac-Toe for the Atari 2600
  • 1979 - Developed the programmable Calculator application
  • 1980 - Published Video Checkers for the Atari 2600
  • 1980 - Left Atari to work at Tndem Computers
  • 1982 - Joined Activison
  • 1982 - Published the biggest game hit River Raid selling more than 1 million cartridges
  • 1983 - Published Happy Trails for the Intellivision
  • 1984 - Returned to Tandem
  • 1990 - Took an early retirement and did voluntary work for the Foresight Institute
  • 2017 - Received the Industry Icon Award at The Game Awards
